► GAMEPLAY

Fortress Section: Navigate walls, shoot destructible ones (pulsing), avoid indestructible walls. Destroy gun tanks.

Space Section: Fight enemy ships in open space. Dodge their fire and shoot them down.

Boss Section: Destroy all blocks ahead of you while the boss advances!

Collectibles: Fuel tanks restore fuel. Powerups grant special abilities (Spread Shot, Rapid Fire, Shield, Slow-Mo).
