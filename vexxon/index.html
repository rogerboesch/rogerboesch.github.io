Fortress Section

  • Navigate through walls with varying heights
  • Destroy pulsing green walls to clear your path
  • Avoid solid green indestructible walls
  • Shoot red gun tanks on the ground
  • Collect fuel tanks to stay airborne
  • Grab powerups for temporary abilities

Powerups

  • Spread Shot - Fire 3 bullets at once (15s)
  • Rapid Fire - Double fire rate (10s)
  • Shield - One-hit protection
  • Slow-Mo - Slow down time (8s)

Tips

  • Watch your fuel gauge - don't run empty!
  • Use altitude to your advantage
  • Destroy enemies quickly for combo bonuses
  • Look ahead - walls appear in the distance

About Vexxon
